# Support policy

This project is maintained for a client and also serves as portfolio material. A lightweight support process is provided below. Contact details use placeholders until the agency is available.

## Channels

- Email (preferred): support@your-agency.tld (placeholder)
- Security issues: security@your-agency.tld (placeholder) — or see SECURITY.md
- General inquiries: contact@your-agency.tld (placeholder)

Replace these addresses with your real agency contacts when available.

## Business hours

- Monday–Friday, 09:00–18:00 (Europe/Paris) — adjust as needed
- Responses outside business hours are best-effort

## Response targets (non-binding, best-effort)

- Critical outage: same business day
- Major bug: 1–3 business days
- Minor bug / enhancements: 3–10 business days

## What to include in a request

- Summary and steps to reproduce
- Expected vs. actual behavior
- Screenshots or console logs if relevant
- Affected URL(s), browser(s), and environment (prod/staging/local)
- Priority/impact (e.g., blocking checkout vs. minor UI)

## Scope

- Frontend defects, a11y/SEO/PWA issues, CI build problems
- Small enhancements that fit within current architecture
- Larger feature work should be discussed and estimated separately

## For internal use (maintainer notes)

- Triage new requests and label them (bug, enhancement, question)
- Confirm reproduction, define acceptance criteria, and schedule
- Keep CHANGELOG.md updated for released changes
